Who We Are

At Glenwood Veterinarian Clinic, we believe pets are family. Our team of dedicated veterinarians, technicians, and animal lovers work together to provide compassionate, high-quality care for every patient who walks, trots, or hops through our doors.

From routine check-ups and preventative care to advanced treatments, we combine modern veterinary medicine with a gentle, personal touch. We know that each pet has a unique story, and we take the time to listen — to you and to them.

Whether it’s helping a new puppy grow strong, guiding an aging cat through their golden years, or caring for unexpected emergencies, Glenwood Veterinarian Clinic is here to support every stage of your pet’s life.

Because at Glenwood, it’s not just about treating animals — it’s about nurturing the bond you share with them.

Our Doctor

Meet Dr. Glenwood,

the heart and namesake of Glenwood Veterinarian Clinic. With over two decades of experience in veterinary medicine, Dr. Glenwood has dedicated his career to improving the lives of animals and strengthening the bonds they share with their families.

Raised on a small farm just outside of town, he learned early on that compassion, patience, and respect for animals are at the core of good care. After earning his Doctorate of Veterinary Medicine, he returned home with a mission: to build a clinic that treats every pet like part of the family.

Whether he’s treating a sprained paw, comforting a worried owner, or welcoming a family’s first pet, Dr. Glenwood’s approach is always rooted in empathy and understanding. His patients may not be able to speak, but he’s made a career out of listening — to their behavior, their needs, and the people who love them.

For Dr. Glenwood, veterinary care isn’t just a job — it’s a lifelong calling rooted in kindness, trust, and community.
