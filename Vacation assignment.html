Favorite Vacation Place

By Gary Sullivan


My name is Gary, and I am a huge nerd for science, computers and video games. I welcome kindness and patience when working with others and I like to learn from them as well. A portion of my interest in science is a specific interest in physics. While I don't plan to professionally persue it as a carreer, I love learning about our physical world, and the harsh laws of physics that everything abides under. Cars, air planes, trains all have their own strengths and weaknesses which compliment each other, and I can talk about them for hours.


My favorite vacation place to visit is Ocean City MD. It offers a ton of things to do like exploring the board walk, relaxing on the beach and swimming in the water, enjoying the Jolly Rogers amusement park at the end of the board walk and much more. My father, mother, brother and I used to go to Ocean City for vacation every year when we were in elementary school. Sometimes, we went during the end of the school year when class was still ongoing.
